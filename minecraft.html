Instructions

You are a minion who desperately wants to get married. The tile your soulmate is hidding in is red-bordered. Your goal is to get to her! To do so, you have to move your minion to the tile which is next to hers. Once you've found her, click on her to marry her ! Your minion can move horizontaly, and can jump on stone tiles. You have many tools to help you! An axe to harvest wood and leafs, a pickaxe to mine stone, and a shovel to dig ground and grass. But if you fell from too high, you will die :(... Build the world to find your way to the one you love !!
